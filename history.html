Why This Initiative Started

Every day restaurants and hotels throw away large amounts of perfectly edible food. At the same time, millions of people struggle to find their daily meals.

FeedPoint was created to solve this problem by connecting restaurants, volunteers, and NGOs to distribute surplus food to people who need it most.

Our goal is simple: reduce food waste and fight hunger at the same time.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to build a strong network of food donors, volunteers, and NGOs that work together to ensure that no food goes to waste.

We believe that food is a basic human right, and through collaboration we can create a sustainable system where excess food reaches those in need.

By using technology and community participation, FeedPoint aims to make food distribution faster and more efficient.

Our Journey

2023

The idea of FeedPoint was created by a small group of volunteers.

2024

We partnered with our first restaurants to collect surplus food.

2025

More than 10,000 meals were distributed to people in need.

Future Goal

Expand our network globally to eliminate food waste worldwide.
